Introduction

Micah George, Computer Engineering Major

Having always been active in robotics-related electives and projects through high school and college, I chose to dissect the Taylor Digital Kitchen Scale (Model 5273821) because it demonstrates sensor interfacing, provides insight into signal processing & digital filtering, contains a microcontroller-based system with an LCD interface, and is useful for learning low-power embedded system design.
